About ChatOps.ai

chatops.ai drives to create industry standards and best practices for the use of ChatOps, automating tasks via natural communication.

Goals

  • Define standards for developing chatbots equipped with machine learning services
  • Provide resources for developers to further develop their chatbots
  • Grow the community of enthusiasts and creators of chatbots that fufill ChatOps
